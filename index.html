2023 Journal

Signal Optimization to Improve Traffic Conditions: A Case Study of Satdobato and Gwarko Intersections, Kathmandu Valley

Rupak Khadka et al.

Nepal Journal of Civil Engineering · Pulchowk Campus, IOE, Tribhuvan University

This study investigates signal optimization strategies at two major congested intersections — Satdobato and Gwarko — in the Kathmandu Valley. Using SIDRA Intersection software, comprehensive traffic counts and demand analysis were conducted to design improved signal phasing and timing plans. The optimized signals significantly reduced average delay, improved intersection capacity, and mitigated congestion in one of Nepal's most traffic-dense urban corridors, offering a replicable framework for signal redesign in developing-country urban contexts.
